Welcome to the Witsuwit'en Hibikinic dictionary webpage

The recorded sound files which accompany the dictionary are hyperlinked to the text on this web page. However, this is not the complete dictionary. For translations of the Witsuwit'en words and sentences you will need to consult the published dictionary. Also, images such as the one below are included only in the published dictionary.

The Witsuwit'en Language and Culture Society has the official website for the dictionary. It points back to this page so that users can listen to native speakers pronouncing some of the dictionary.
